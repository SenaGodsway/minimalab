3. AI MVP in 30 days (realistic scope)

“Can we build an AI MVP?” Yes—if you scope it correctly.

An AI MVP that ships fast usually has:
- one user role (maybe two)
- one core workflow
- one success metric (time saved, tickets reduced, conversion uplift)
- strong human-in-the-loop plus audit trail

What it usually doesn’t have: 12 integrations on day one, perfect data, or a plan to “add security later.”
